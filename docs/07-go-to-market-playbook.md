# MarketAxis AI — Go-To-Market Playbook

## Your Marketing (in Plain English)

You're marketing to developers. Developers are special because:
- They **hate** being marketed to
- They **love** products that genuinely solve problems
- They trust **transparency** and **build-in-public** content
- They hang out in **specific places** online

So your GTM is: **Be genuine. Show your work. Be where they are.**

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–4 of building)

### Build in Public
- Tweet your daily progress on X (#buildinpublic)
- Share screenshots, architecture decisions, wins and struggles
- Post weekly updates on IndieHackers

### Build a Waitlist
- Simple landing page: headline + description + email signup
- Offer: "Early access + lifetime discount for first 100 signups"
- Collect: email, role (dev/founder/agency), biggest marketing pain

### Seed Communities
- Join: r/startups, r/SideProject, r/indiehackers, r/Entrepreneur
- Contribute genuinely first (answer questions, share insights)
- Don't spam your product

### Content to create:
| # | Title | Channel |
|---|-------|---------|
| 1 | "Why developers suck at marketing (and how AI can help)" | Blog / Dev.to |
| 2 | "I'm building an AI marketing team — here's week 1" | X Thread |
| 3 | "The #1 reason indie apps fail (it's not the code)" | LinkedIn |
| 4 | "Behind the scenes: building AI agents with Composio" | X Thread |
| 5 | "What happens after you ship? Most devs have no idea" | Reddit |

## Phase 2: Beta Launch (Weeks 5–6)

### Invite beta users
- Email waitlist in batches (50 at a time)
- Personal DMs to developers you've engaged with
- Post in communities: "Looking for 50 beta testers"

### Collect feedback
- In-app: quick 3-question survey after first strategy generation
- Weekly: 15-min calls with 5 users
- Discord/Slack: private beta channel for real-time feedback

### Key questions:
1. "Did MarketAxis give you a strategy you'd actually use?"
2. "What's missing?"
3. "Would you pay for this? How much?"

| Day +7 | Week 1 recap + learnings | IndieHackers |

### Product Hunt Tips
- Launch on Tuesday or Wednesday (avoid weekends)
- Prepare: logo, tagline, description, 4 screenshots, maker comment
- Rally your network: ask 20 people to upvote + comment (genuine)
- Reply to every comment within 30 min
- Post your PH link on X/LinkedIn with a personal story

### Hacker News Tips
- Title: "Show HN: MarketAxis AI – Autonomous marketing for developers"
- Keep first comment factual, technical, honest
- Don't ask for upvotes (against rules)
- Be ready to answer technical questions

## Content Marketing Framework

### The "Teach, Don't Sell" Approach

Every piece of content should follow this ratio:
- **80% value** (teach marketing to developers)
- **20% product** (show how MarketAxis helps)

### Content Pillars
1. **"Marketing for Developers"** — educational content
2. **"Build in Public"** — your journey building MarketAxis
3. **"User Success Stories"** — how users grew their apps
4. **"AI & Marketing"** — thought leadership

### SEO Keywords to Target
| Keyword | Intent | Difficulty |
|---------|--------|------------|
| "how to market an app" | Educational | Medium |

| "AI marketing tool for developers" | Commercial | Low |
| "indie app marketing" | Educational | Low |
| "product hunt launch strategy" | Educational | Medium |
| "app store optimization guide" | Educational | Medium |

## Referral Program

### Mechanics
- User shares unique referral link
- Referred user signs up → referrer gets 1 month free
- Referred user also gets 1 month free (both-sided)
- Display referral count in dashboard

### Why This Works for Developers
- Developers share tools in communities
- "Word of mouth" is the #1 growth channel for dev tools
- Low friction: just share a link
