# MarketAxis AI — Pricing & Monetization Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

> **Price for value, not for features.**
> Your users don't care about "number of API calls." They care about "does this save me 10+ hours/month?"

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier — "Try It"
- **Price:** $0/month
- **What's included:**
  - 1 app profile
  - Strategy generation (1x)
  - 5 content pieces/month
  - No social posting
  - No launch kit
- **Purpose:** Let users experience the magic. Hook them.

### Indie Tier — "For Builders"
- **Price:** $19/month (or $190/year — save 17%)
- **What's included:**
  - 1 app profile
  - Unlimited strategy regeneration
  - 30 content pieces/month
  - 1 social account connected
  - Basic launch kit
  - Assist mode only
- **Target:** Side project builders, hobby devs

### Startup Tier — "For Growth" ⭐ (Most Popular)
- **Price:** $49/month (or $490/year)
- **What's included:**
  - 3 app profiles
  - Unlimited strategy + content
  - 5 social accounts connected
  - Full launch kit
  - Assist + Autopilot mode
  - Social reply suggestions
  - Priority support
- **Target:** Solo founders, small startups

### Pro Tier — "For Scale"
- **Price:** $149/month (or $1,490/year)
- **What's included:**
  - Unlimited app profiles
  - Everything in Startup
  - Advanced analytics
  - Outreach automation
  - Browser automation
  - Custom brand training
  - Dedicated support
- **Target:** Agencies, multi-product teams

---

## Early Adopter Strategy

### Lifetime Deal (First 100 Users)
- **Price:** $149 one-time for Startup tier (lifetime)
- **Why:** Creates urgency, builds initial user base, generates cash
- **Where to sell:** AppSumo, your own site, IndieHackers

### Beta Pricing
- Free for beta testers (first 50 users)
- They give feedback → you fix issues → they become advocates
- After beta, offer 50% off first 3 months

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CAC (paid) | < $30 |

| LTV/CAC ratio | > 3:1 |

At $49/mo → 90–96% gross margin ✅
